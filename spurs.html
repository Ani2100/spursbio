San Antonio Spurs

The San Antonio spurs are a American professional basketball team in the NBA.
They compete in the Western Conference SouthWest Divison.
They have won 5 Nba championships placing them as the fifth most team in the NBA with the most titles.
The spurs are famous for their beautiful team style of play, their playstyle includes terrific ball movement and team work backed up by extraordinary coaching.

Fun Facts!

  • The spurs are tied for the longest consecutive playoff appearances (22) from 1998-2019
  • The Spurs have only missed the playoffs 4 times since they joined the NBA in 1976!
  • The Spurs used to be called the Dallas Chapparals
  • The spurs are the only franchise with a winning record against every NBA team
  • The Spurs have a all time .618% win percentage, spurs have the highest win percentage of all time in the NBA.
  • The legendary spurs big three (Tim Duncan, Manu Ginobili, and Tony Parker) have won 575 regular season games and 126 postseason(playoffs) games together.
  • From 1999-2016 (17 seasons) the spurs have won 50 or more games in every season of the total 82 games in an nba regular season.
